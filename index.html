About University Communications

University Communications leads both outbound and internal communications with the university’s many constituencies, including current and prospective students, faculty and staff, alumni, government and public officials, and the generalpublic – working in close coordination with staff communicators in Athletics, Alumni Relations and other departments and schools.

Our staff provides expertise in the following areas:

Strategic Communications

Through the development of strategic marketing and communications plans, the Public Relations team works with campus partners to produce and gain attention for the many outstanding students, faculty, staff members, research projects, events and initiatives on the main Ole Miss campus as well as our satellite sites in Southaven, Tupelo, Booneville, Jackson and Grenada.

Media Relations

The PR group serves as the liaison between campus partners and a wide range of broadcast, print and digital media outlets at the local, state and national level.

Video and Digital Strategy

The Public Relations team also includes resources for video production and content development for the university’s web and social media intiatives.

Printing and Graphic Design

The Printing and Graphic Design team works closely with other members of the University Communications division to produce high-quality print and digital pieces for a variety of purposes, including marketing, alumni communications, on-campus promotions and national advertising.

Photography

The Photography team provides award-winning images to the Public Relations team, the Printing and Graphic Design team and to campus partners on a daily basis and are the talent behind the Ole Miss home page images as well as the university’s Flikr account.
